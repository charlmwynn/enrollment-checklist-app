Student Financial Services

How to Apply

Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) to be automatically considered for federal, state, and Lasell University need-based aid. Lasell University's FAFSA Code: 002158.

Our priority FAFSA deadline is March 31. Submitting after this date may reduce eligibility for some aid programs. For Massachusetts residents, the FAFSA must be submitted by May 1 to be considered for the Mass Grant.

Start Your FAFSA

Financial Aid Awards

After Lasell receives your FAFSA, our Office of Financial Aid reviews your information to prepare an aid offer. Awards may include:

  • Lasell University Awards -- merit‑based scholarships
  • Institutional Grants -- need‑based Lasell grant funding
  • Federal and State Grants -- Pell, SEOG, etc.
  • Loans -- Federal Direct Loans and other loan programs
  • Work-Study -- on-campus employment offered as part of your financial aid package

All admitted students are automatically considered for Lasell University Awards during the admission review.

Net Price Calculator

Want to estimate your costs after financial aid? Use Lasell’s Net Price Calculator to get an early idea of what you might pay.

Important Considerations

If your family’s financial situation has changed (e.g., job loss, reduced income), please reach out to our Office of Financial Aid. We may be able to review your circumstances and make adjustments.

You are expected to maintain Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) in order to continue receiving aid. Make sure to understand these requirements.
